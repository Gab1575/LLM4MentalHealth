# LLM4MentalHealth
This project is the development of 2 non-anthropomorphic desktop companion social robots. A Flower and a Pet Rock. This is connected with a research study aimed at using LLM's for Mental health. 


## Flower Power
### Description
Flower Power is a social robot consititing of two main parts: A flower head and a continuum robot stem. 
- The flower head is generally based off of a daisy. 
    - It has 5 petals that are all individually colour controallable through addressible RGB LED's
    - The petals angle's are controllable, allowing a range of motion from a full open to close
    - The flower head contains a rotation joint, allowing for a nodding motion to be controlled.

    These different control paths allows the robot to express different emotions through movement and colour. 

- Continuum Robot Flower stem
    The stem of the flower is controllable and can produce organic movements through a 2 segement tendon driven continuum robot. 

With both of these systems, the robot can be a non-verbal companion capable of expression emotions and passing along ideas and recommendations to user to support their habits and mental health.
